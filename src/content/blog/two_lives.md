# Two Lives
Two Lives is an interactive audiovisual installation exploring the concept of life through two parallel systems: artificial life and natural life.

The artificial life module combines ALife-based visual simulation with a custom concatenative synthesiser, allowing visual behaviours to directly shape sound generation in real time. The natural life module consists of a Max patch presenting microscopic footage of human fertilised eggs while generating melodies, together with an Ableton Live performance system driven by MIDI from Max. Both modules incorporate gesture-based interaction, enabling audiences to manipulate sound and visuals through hand movements.
