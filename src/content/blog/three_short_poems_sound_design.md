## Three Short Poems Sound Design
These are the English translations of three of my early short poems. Using only a limited set of materials, I created the sound design for each poem. The process felt like creating something from 0 to 1, rather than from 1 to 10.

1. Neon Lights</br>
The city lights up the night.</br>
Neon lights stream into my room,</br>
And burn my home to ash.</br>

2. Sunset</br>
The sunset has packed its bags,</br>
Ready to go.</br>
I’ve thought about going home too.</br>

3. Vine Film</br>
It’s a no-entry, long-abandoned corridor.</br>
Golden railings now overgrown with lush green vines,</br>
A winding, suspended place hanging mid-air.</br>
In the sunlight, I glimpse green sprung from dirty patches of mud.</br>
